Multi-Agent Financial AI Platform

Welcome to our open-source financial analysis platform powered by AI agents. This system provides market data, technical analysis, sentiment analysis, and automated reporting.

Technical Analysis

Our technical analysis agent calculates key indicators:

  • Moving Averages (SMA/EMA): Identify trends over specific time periods
  • RSI (Relative Strength Index): Measure momentum and identify overbought/oversold conditions
  • MACD: Identify changes in strength, direction, momentum, and duration of trends
  • Bollinger Bands: Measure volatility and identify potential breakouts

Sentiment Analysis

Our NLP agent analyzes financial news and social media to determine market sentiment:

  • News Processing: Analyze articles from financial publications
  • Sentiment Scoring: Quantify positive, neutral, or negative sentiment
  • Trend Identification: Track changing sentiment over time
  • Market Correlation: See how sentiment relates to price movements

Data Agent

Our data agent retrieves and manages financial market data:

  • Real-time & Historical Prices: Access current and past market data
  • Multiple Sources: Integrate with open financial APIs
  • Time-Series Storage: Efficiently store and query historical data
  • Automatic Updates: Stay current with latest market information
